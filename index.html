About Divine Endowments

Divine Endowments Limited is one of the leading and fastest-growing fashion houses in Nigeria, with a clear mission: to deliver perfect fitting in bespoke and ready-to-wear garments for today’s discerning customer. From our base in Ikeja and airport outlets, we serve clients who want premium African fashion that feels as good as it looks.

We specialise in African designer gowns, kaftans, blouses, trousers and more for both men and women. Every piece is crafted with attention to detail, neat finishing and quality fabrics, whether you’re dressing for owambe, travel, the boardroom or casual days out.

Over the years, Divine Endowments has become a trusted name in Lagos and beyond, redefining African ready-wear and making contemporary, well-tailored pieces accessible to everyone.
